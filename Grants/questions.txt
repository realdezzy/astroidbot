Milestones are how your grant pays out — the grants team verifies each one before its payment releases.

Strong milestones are:

Shippable outcomes, not activities (“v1 live on mainnet,” not “work on v1”).
Independently verifiable — a link, a metric, or a deployed contract someone can check.
Sized for roughly 4–8 weeks of work each.
