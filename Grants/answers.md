# AstroidBot Stacks Grant Application Answers

## Project Description
A functional automated trading assistant and dashboard for the Stacks blockchain, supporting ALEX and Bitflow pools. The grant funds the hosted deployment setup, closed and public beta testing, security audits, and community launch for retail users.

## Step 3: Audience and Ecosystem Fit

### Primary Audience
Retail DeFi users, day traders, and mainstream Stacks ecosystem participants who want automated trading and portfolio management.

### Audience Segmentation
- **Retail DeFi Users:** Mainstream users looking for hands-off dollar-cost averaging (DCA), grid trading automation, and automated portfolio rebalancing with zero setup.
- **Active Traders:** Normal users requiring limit orders and fast execution across multiple DEX pools.

### Why Stacks?
Stacks is the leading layer for Bitcoin DeFi. AstroidBot leverages Stacks' fast block times, security, and DEX liquidity to offer seamless automated trading.

### Maintenance Plan
The platform will be maintained by the core team. We will resolve user issues, perform regular maintenance, and release updates based on ecosystem needs and user feedback.

### Ecosystem Fit
AstroidBot provides essential automation—DCA, limit orders, and rebalancing—which currently requires manual trading. It makes Stacks DeFi accessible to non-technical users, driving trade volume and user retention.

## Step 6: Track-Specific Context (Getting Started)

### What are you proposing to explore or build?
AstroidBot: A non-custodial automated trading assistant for the Stacks blockchain accessible via a web dashboard and Telegram bot.

### What user or ecosystem problem motivates the project?
The Stacks ecosystem lacks unified, user-friendly trading automation. Users currently have to execute trades manually, which is highly inefficient for systematic strategies like DCA or portfolio rebalancing. AstroidBot automates these processes.

### Why is Stacks the right environment for this work?
Stacks brings smart contract capabilities directly to Bitcoin, enabling automation of Stacks tokens and Bitcoin-linked assets.

### What have you already validated, prototyped, or learned?
We built a functional prototype integrating ALEX, Bitflow, and Velar. We verified that users want a zero-setup, immediate-access web dashboard and Telegram interface rather than self-hosting the code.

### Who will do the work and what experience do they bring?
A full-stack developer with experience building user-friendly Web3 dashboards and integrations.

### What is the smallest useful outcome this grant should produce?
A publicly accessible version of AstroidBot where anyone can log in and start automated DCA and rebalancing strategies immediately with zero technical setup.

### What evidence will show the concept is worth continuing?
User registration numbers, active strategy count, and transaction volume executed through the platform.

### What dependencies or risks could affect delivery?
Latency or contract updates from third-party DEXs.

### What support from the Stacks ecosystem would help?
Introductions to Stacks wallet and DeFi teams for integration feedback and collaboration.

### How will you share progress or learnings publicly?
Public repository access, project updates on X, and guides in Stacks community channels.

### What happens after the grant if the work succeeds?
We will launch advanced features, integrate sBTC, and scale the hosted platform to support more users.

### Any other context reviewers should consider?
The core logic is already mature and heavily tested, ensuring a fast and secure public launch.
